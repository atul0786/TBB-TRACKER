🔗 Google Sheet Connect Karein

Apni Google Apps Script Web App URL yahan paste karke "Connect" dabayein — ek baar karne ke baad hamesha yaad rahega (is browser me), dobara paste karne ki zaroorat nahi. Har page par ye dobara karne ki zaroorat nahi — ye setting poori app ke liye ek hi baar hoti hai.

Kaise kaam karta hai

Chhota sa reminder.
  • Ye connection sirf ek baar karni hai — is browser me hamesha ke liye save ho jaati hai
  • Har page/tab ke upar-right corner me ek chhota 🔄 Refresh button hamesha milega — naya data dekhna ho to sirf wahi dabayein
  • Poori file dobara kholne ya is Connection tab par wapas aane ki zaroorat nahi refresh ke liye
  • Kisi doosre Google Sheet se connect karna ho to yahan wapas aakar "Change URL" use karein
